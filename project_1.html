E-Commerce DAC Store

This project is designing a website application for the DAC store. DAC Store is located at Perum Taman lasrana RT07 / 22, Sleman Regency, Yogyakarta is one example of a company that is engaged in fashion. This shop has branches spread across several areas of the city of Yogyakarta.

The Problem

The problem that exists in the DAC Store at this time is that store management that still uses a conventional system, there are often problems in the store in terms of marketing the sales of goods for a wider market such as marketing goods to customers who are outside the city or inside the city, promotional media used by stores only using social media such as Instagram and Facebook. This is because the delivery of information about sales in stores has not been able to keep up with the world of online markets with a wider range of access by customers.

I design the homepage design from the draft and develop it. Then i also design all the inner pages. The list of the pages that i designed was :
  1. Homepage
  2. Category
  3. Chart
  4. Checkout
  5. History
  6. Login
  7. Logout

Homepage



Main page is the page to display the products provided by the dac store. currently dac store has several products and many types.

Category



Category is a page where you can search for specific products so that customers can easily find them.

Chart



Chart is a page to display product data that will be purchased by customers.

Checkout



Checkout is a page that displays product data that will be paid by customers.

History



History is a page that displays a list of customer purchases history.
