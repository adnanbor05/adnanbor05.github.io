Jowey Farm


This project is designing a website application for Jowey farm and Jowey Store. Jowey farm is a modern organic farming company that produces organic vegetables, organic fruit and other organic foods that have been certified so that their quality is guaranteed. Our location in Taman Sari, Ciapus - Bogor is ideal for agricultural growth, especially vegetables. Jowey farm is managed professionally and with modern farming methods with Green House to ensure our products are the best premium organic vegetables, fresh, delicious and nutritious.
Jowey farm has obtained organic certification and we are currently focusing on growing 3 kinds of vegetables, namely Kale Nero, Romaine Lettuce and Beef Tomato. In line with our mission to help build the nation with organic food and vegetables, we are also active in helping the children around, especially orphans so that they can also grow and develop into the hope of the nation.
Jowey farm will continue to move forward and become the best. Product quality, correct delivery and consistency as well as service are our priorities to match customer expectations. You can entrust your vegetable needs to us.

The Problem

The website is built to provide information about hydroponic plants and can sell. Now that they are growing bigger, they need to showcase their website to potential clients and customers. So they need a better content structure and of course a better and fresher and more attractive design.

Fiture

I designed the veranda from drafts and built it as attractive as possible. Then I also designed the whole page. the list of pages I designed is:
  1. Home
  2. About
  3. Product
  4. Ivent
  5. Media
  6. Contact Us
  7. Account

Home



Home page is the main page of the website.

About



Page About is a page that displays about the company, the history of the company, the vision and mission the company will achieve.

Product



A business page is a page that is used to display information about the products the company sells. on this page there is a dropdown menu option that displays several product categories to display.

Ivent



Event page is a page that displays information about events managed by jowey farm. This page shows some information about the ivent and some photos about the ivent.

Media



Media page is a page that displays some photo documentation of the activities held by the company.

Contact Us



The contact us page is a page that displays form information that can be used to send messages to companies.
