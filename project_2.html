Inventory Management System

This project is designing a website application for the Balakosa coffe shop. Balakosa is located at Balakosa Coffe & Co yang berlokasi di jl. Kedawung, Nologaten, Caturtunggal, Depok, Sleman, Yogyakarta.

The Problem

The main problem faced in managing inventory in Balakosa is when goods are stored that exceeds the target demand, it can increase the costs incurred by the trading company so that it is not optimal and increases warehouse storage costs with the risk of depreciation. Conversely, saving costs can be done by reducing the inventory of goods, but this solution can result in a lack of supply of goods (out of stock), goods that are required to wait for the next order to return and wait for a sales transaction.
