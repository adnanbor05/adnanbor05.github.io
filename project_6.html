PT. Beruang Salju


This project is designing a website application for PT. Beruang Salju. PT. Snow Bear is a company from the Surya Jawara Group that focuses on producing cleaning products that are safe for health.
Our company has a business strategy of creating international quality products at competitive prices.

The Problem

This web site was built due to requests from companies. The company wants a website that can display information about products that have been managed by the company and its managers through a website.

Fiture

I designed the veranda from drafts and built it as attractive as possible. Then I also designed the whole page. the list of pages I designed is:
  1. Home
  2. Product
  3. Article
  4. Order
  5. About Us
  6. Gallery
  7. Contact

Home



Home page is the main page of the website beruangsalju.

Product



Page About is a page that displays about the product.

Article
